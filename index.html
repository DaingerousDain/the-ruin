The Ruin Experience

Nestled among coconut trees on the iconic Hiriketiya Bay, The Ruin is more than a restaurant—it's a day-to-night destination for travelers seeking beauty, flavor, and relaxation.

Iconic Sunset Views

Our prime location offers arguably the most breathtaking sunset panorama in Sri Lanka. Watch the sky ignite with color over the Indian Ocean from your daybed or dinner table.

Gourmet Beachside Dining

Led by an international chef, our kitchen fuses authentic Mexican flavors with the freshest local seafood and produce. Every taco, ceviche, and cocktail is made from scratch.

Vibrant Yet Relaxed Vibe

Set amidst atmospheric ruins and swaying palms, The Ruin strikes the perfect balance. Enjoy chilled-out days on premium sunbeds and lively, magical evenings under the stars.
